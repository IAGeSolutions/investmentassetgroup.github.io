Who We Are

IAG Energy Solutions is a Houston-based energy trading and logistics firm focused on sourcing and delivering ultra-low sulfur diesel (ULSD) and refined products from the United States to customers in Mexico with a strong emphasis on risk management and operational reliability.

Energy & Logistics

What We Do

IAG Energy Solutions focuses on structured fuel supply for commercial and industrial buyers, with an emphasis on ULSD deliveries from the U.S. into Mexico.

ULSD & Refined Product Sourcing

  • Identification of reliable ULSD and refined product supply in the U.S. market
  • Commercial negotiation with producers, suppliers, and wholesalers
  • Matching volumes, timing, and specifications to customer requirements

Cross-Border Delivery & Logistics Coordination

  • Coordination with logistics providers for transport and border crossing
  • Structuring delivered-to-destination options where appropriate
  • Alignment of documentation for customs and compliance

Structured Commercial & Credit Terms

  • Design of commercial terms to support factoring and trade finance
  • Clear invoicing flows between U.S. sellers, IAG, and Mexican buyers
  • Attention to credit exposure, payment terms, and collection risk

Risk Management & Market Insight

  • Experience with energy risk analytics and trading data
  • Monitoring of price exposure and margin requirements
  • Support for structured or hedged pricing where applicable
